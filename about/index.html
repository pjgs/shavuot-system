About Us

Shavuot System — Simple, Fast, Efficient

Who We Are

Shavuot System is an integrated agricultural management platform designed to connect producers, companies, and educational institutions in a single ecosystem. We believe in making agricultural technology accessible, simple, and effective.

Our Mission

To provide precise, reliable, and easy-to-use tools that help agricultural producers and organizations make better decisions, improve their productivity, and grow sustainably.

Our Vision

To become the leading agricultural intelligence platform in Latin America, driving the digital transformation of the agricultural sector through innovation and education.

What We Offer

Shavuot HOME — The central hub connecting all modules of our ecosystem.

Shavuot PRO — A directory of companies committed to agricultural progress, with tools for producers to find trusted partners.

Shavuot EDU — Educational cycles, courses, and resources for agronomists, technicians, and producers looking to grow professionally.

Shavuot LAB — Advanced analytics and traceability tools for crop monitoring and management.

Our Values

Precision: Every recommendation is based on data and science.
Simplicity: Powerful tools don't need to be complicated.
Integrity: We act with transparency in every interaction.
Innovation: We constantly evolve to serve our users better.
